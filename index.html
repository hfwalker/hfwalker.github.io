hi, i'm hilary!

I'm a UX/UI Designer who loves to create order out of chaos. I'm in my element when I have a problem to solve!

I'm passionate about user-centered design and building beautiful and intuitive products that make people's lives better. Using my natural curiosity, I strive to get to know and deeply understand the user and I will advocate for them throughout the design process.

It's important to me that I have an opportunity to learn something new every day, which is just one of the reasons why I love being a member of close and collaborative teams that encourage continual growth.

When I'm not behind a desk, you can find me in the mountains, usually pointing out sweet waterfalls...
